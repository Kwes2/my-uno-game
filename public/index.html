The Laws of the Forest

Goal: Be the tree with the deepest Roots after 4 Ages.

The Mechanics: Each Age consists of 5 Rounds. Every round, you play a card to give nutrients to a rival.

  • Sapling Height: If you receive nutrients, your height increases.
  • Shading (Marks): At the start of an Age, you "Mark" a rival. If that rival is the unique shortest tree at the end of the Age, you gain +10 Root Depth.
  • The Network (Hunger): The forest demands a specific nutrient (Sticks, Leaves, or Resin). The player who contributes the most of this type to the network gains +15 Root Depth. The lowest contributor loses -8.
  • Defying (Value 0): Playing a 0-value card blocks all incoming nutrients for that round.

End of Age: Your Root Depth increases by your total Sapling Height.
